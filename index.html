Introduction to Marketing

What is Marketing?

Marketing is a process by which companies create value for customers and build strong customer relationships to capture value from customers in return.

Marketing Process

The marketing process involves understanding customer needs, designing customer-driven strategies, constructing marketing programs, building relationships, and capturing value.

Understanding Customer Needs

Needs: States of deprivation including:

  • Physical needs: food, clothing, warmth, safety
  • Social needs: belonging and affection
  • Individual needs: knowledge and self-expression

Wants: The form that needs take as they are shaped by culture and individual personality.

Demands: Wants backed by buying power.

Marketing Myopia

Marketing myopia is the mistake of focusing only on existing wants and losing sight of underlying consumer needs.
